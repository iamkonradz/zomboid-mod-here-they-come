title=Here They Come!
description=Here They Come (beta): An immersive horde event experience!
description=
description=What does it do?
description=This mod introduces horde events to Zomboid! These events are meant to remain immersive, and highly configurable.
description=Configuration range from their spread, size, variation, and trigger time frame.
description=
description=Over time, zombies get restless and excited by your scent. After a certain threshold is reached, a horde of zombies will come hunting you down.
description=The event will also trigger an invisible sound pulse that will draw both existing and horde zombies towards the player (this behavior can be adjusted and turned off).
description=
description=How do I customize my experience?
description=There are many sandbox variables that can help you tailor the experience to your liking:
description=	
description=    - Cooldown: Cooldown between two hordes in game minutes
description=	- Min Starting Hour: Min hour of day at which the Horde may happen
description=    - Max Starting Hour: Max hour of day at which the Horde may happend
description=    - Minimum hourly progress: Minimum amount of agitation per hour
description=    - Maximum hourly progress: Maximum amount of agitation per hour
description=    - Trigger threshold: Horde agitation trigger threshold
description=    - First day: First day at which a horde may appear
description=    - Number of waves: Number of zombie waves per horde
description=    - Minimum zombies: Minimum amount of zombies per wave
description=    - Maximum zombies: Maximum amount of zombies per wave
description=    - Additional Zombies per horde: Additional number of maximum zombies for each horde
description=    - Time between waves: Number of minutes between waves
description=    - Minimum spawn distance: Minimum wave spawn distance
description=    - Maximum horde spawn distance: Maximum wave spawn distance
description=    - Horde Angle Spread: Max angle at which the wave origin may be focused
description=    - Zombies per spawn batch: Number of zombies per spawn batch
description=    - Spawn Rate: Delay in ticks between spawn batches
description=    - Horde progress indicator: Whether to display the horde agitation icon or not
description=    - Heads-up text: Displays a warning text when horde starts/progresses
description=    - Heads-up time: Number of minutes after the event before the first wave starts
description=    - Noise pulse on player: Silently pulse and draw zombies to player during Horde
description=    - Pulse Range: Range of the pulse pulling zombies to players
description=    - Noise Pulse Frequency: How many minutes between noise pulses
description=
description=Will it work on multiplayer?
description=This mod was designed with MP in mind, so: YES!
description=
description=Where and how will the zombies spawn?
description=They currently spawn outside only, in adjustable batches, and in several waves. Each player in an area will increase the amount of spawned zombies. Their direction is random, but will be consistent across players. The spread can be adjusted so they may also come from every direction.
description=
description=What kind of zombies will spawn?
description=Zombies will have their outfits adequately adjusted according to their spawn biome.
description=
description=Is it safe to add to an existing save?
description=Yes.
